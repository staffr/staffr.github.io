What is Staffr?

Staffr aims to help consulting firms in staffing projects for their consultants. Every member of a firm posseses a unique skillset, and working with the skills provided by each unique consultant can be time consuming. By using staffr, each consultants expertise as well as their past projects are right at your fingertips, and managing projects becomes a breeze.

Designed for project staffers

Staffr is a software that is designed for you. We understand how difficult it is to match the right consultant to the right project.

Time saver

Staffr saves alot of time. We understand how time consuming project staffing can be, and make the process more efficient.

UX-centred

You will never be lost using Staffr. We have carefully architected our software to work around you, exclusively for you. Have at it!

Mobile-friendly

Staffr is a software that is cross platform. Not only are we available on the web, but we are avaiable on Android and iOS as well.

Easy to customize

Staffr can be customized to fit the needs of any project staffer. Sort by skill, experience, or anything that pertains to your project.

LESS files included

Staffr is a software that is big on minimizing data to reduce run time on the server. We don't overload the server, and keep things running.
